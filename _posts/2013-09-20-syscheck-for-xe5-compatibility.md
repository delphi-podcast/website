Well you can check out the <a href="http://delphi.org/2013/09/delphi-xe5-android-device-compatibility/">compatibility lists</a>, or download <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ss.syscheck">SysCheck by Christopher Moeller</a>.

When you run it the first line tells you if you have an <strong>ARMv7</strong>, second line is <strong>Android OS version</strong>, and 3rd line is if you have <strong>NEON instruction</strong> support. Those are the dependencies of <a href="http://www.embarcadero.com/products/delphi">Delphi XE5</a> on Android. The rest of the lines are extra details for your information.

When you run it your output should look something like the following. If Â those three lines check out then your device is ready for Delphi XE5 development.
