<a href="http://community.embarcadero.com/blogs/entry/join-our-club-of-elite-embarcadero-developers-show-us-your-cool-apps"><img class="alignright" src="http://i1.wp.com/delphi.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/Cool_App_Contest_Logo_and_Graphic.png?zoom=2&amp;resize=206%2C198" alt="Embarcadero Cool App Contest" width="206" height="198" /></a>A few years ago my wife and I flew to Denmark to meet <a href="http://delphi.org/2016/04/mvp-of-the-week-jens-fudge/">Jens Fudge</a> and I had the great opportunity to speak at the annual <a href="http://www.dapug.dk/">DAPUG</a> 2 day event. My first trip to Denmark was a great treat. This brings me to the next contender in the <a href="http://community.embarcadero.com/blogs/entry/join-our-club-of-elite-embarcadero-developers-show-us-your-cool-apps">Embarcadero Cool App Contest</a>: Rain in Denmark by <a href="http://www.dhigroup.com/">DHI Group</a>.

<a href="https://itunes.apple.com/dk/app/rain-in-denmark/id1030887083?mt=8"><img class="alignleft wp-image-2508" src="http://delphi.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/RainInDenmark.png" alt="Rain in Denmark" width="200" height="200" /></a>
<blockquote>A complete (Windows , OS X, iOS &amp;  Android) cross platform suite of apps to access the Local Area Weather Radars installed here in Denmark by DHI Group. "This app provides you with up to date information on rain in parts of Denmark using DHI’s Local Area Weather Radar (LAWR). You can animate the radar images, load data from specific period, see plots of mean area precipitation for selected calculation areas and more."</blockquote>
<strong>Rain in Denmark</strong> is built with <a href="https://www.embarcadero.com/products/rad-studio">RAD Studio</a> using <a href="https://www.embarcadero.com/products/delphi">Delphi</a>, <a href="http://docwiki.embarcadero.com/RADStudio/en/Creating_a_FireMonkey_Application">FireMonkey</a>, <a href="http://www.tmssoftware.com/site/webgmaps.asp">TMS WebGMaps</a>, <a href="http://www.remotingsdk.com/ro/">RemObjects SDK</a> and <a href="https://www.embarcadero.com/products/appanalytics">AppAnalytics</a> with support for Windows, OS X, iOS &amp; Android.

Check out the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KmA7C1QdfC0">video</a>, or download the app and check it out!
