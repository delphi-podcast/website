My good friend and Delphi MVP <a href="http://wiert.me/">Jeroen Pluimers</a> has just posted an experimental build of <a href="https://bitbucket.org/jeroenp/gexperts-formatter/downloads">GExperts for XE5</a>. He is working with Erik Berry to get an official build up on the <a href="http://www.gexperts.org/">GExperts site</a>.

GExperts is a collection of open source productivity enhancements for Delphi.
